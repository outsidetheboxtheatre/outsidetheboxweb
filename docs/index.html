Propose a Production

All current Sewanee students are welcome to submit a proposal to produce a show with Outside the Box.

Our main stage is the Studio Theatre in the Tennessee Williams Center. Please contact us for more information if you would like to stage your production in a different venue on campus.

If you have an other questions or comments please contact us directly at outsidetheboxtheatregroup@gmail.com.
